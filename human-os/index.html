OPERATING SYSTEMS FOR BEING HUMAN

Free. Evergreen. Cited. 28 pages covering nervous system regulation, ND parenting, difficult conversations, and decision-making — sourced strictly from the published guides.

28 PAGES LIVE 3 EDITORIAL PILLARS 100+ PEER-REVIEWED CITATIONS

Most of what humans struggle with comes from not understanding how humans actually operate. The Human OS Wiki is a free, evergreen reference. Every page covers one specific human-life problem with the same shape: what's happening, why it's happening, and a five-step protocol to do something about it.

The wiki is sourced strictly from the books — Self-Care, Survival Blueprint, Difficult Conversations, Wired to Win, Invisible Disability. No motivational filler. No common-knowledge guesses. Every claim cites the work it came from. If a page makes a claim, the chapter is on the bookshelf.

New pages ship as new books and chapters land. Drop your email at the bottom and we'll send each addition as it goes live.
